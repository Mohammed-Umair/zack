About KASHMIR

There are a lot of hill station in India but whenever One thinks of the most beautiful among them One thinks of Kashmir . Kashmir is a beautiful valley having mountains all sides.The houseboats in Dal Lake are the most visited places in Srinagar. The natural water floats make the food tastier. Srinagar is safe to visit and spend time with family in the natural atmosphere. The Ladakh part of Jammu and Kashmir has the majority of Buddhists. Kashmiri and Urdu are the languages used in Srinagar.Jammu & Kashmir is famous for its apple, anchards and saffron cultivation.Dusing winter, they use Kangri to Keep thermselves warm.
